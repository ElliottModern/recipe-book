Belgian Waffles

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs, separated

  • 2 cups flour

  • 1/4 cup sugar

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1 tesspoon salt

  • 1-1/3 cups milk

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Beat egg whites until stiff. Set aside.

  2. In mixing bowl, combine dry incredients.

  3. In separate bowl, beat milk, egg yolks, and melted butter together.

  4. Add to dry ingredients. Beat until batter is smooth.

  5. Bake until brown and crisp.
