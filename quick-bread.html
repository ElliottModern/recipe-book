Quick Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 800g bread flour

  • 680g warm water

  • 12g active dry yeast

  • 22g salt

Steps:

  1. Add flour, yeast, and salt to a large bowl. Mix evenly.

  2. Add warm water. Mix together. Let sit 2-3 hours covered in bowl.

  3. Preheat oven to 450F 30 minutes before done rising.

  4. When finished rising, remove from bowl onto a floured work surface. Fold edges into itself to form a bottom.

  5. Flip dough over. Turn and stretch bottom to form boul.

  6. Place boul into cast iron and bake for 30 minutes covered.

  7. Remove top of cast iron and cook for 15 minutes.

  8. Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool for 10 minutes.
