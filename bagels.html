Quick Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 297g warm water

  • 10g active dry yeast

  • 23g sugar

  • 500g bread flour

  • 6g salt

Steps:

  1. Combine warm water, yeast, and sugar in a small side bowl. Let rest for 10 minutes.

  2. Add bread flour, salt, and yeast mixture to a large bowl. Mix together until dough is shaggy.

  3. Turn dough onto a work surface and knead for 10 minutes. When surface is silky, fold sides into itself, place in a well oiled bowl and cover. Let rise for 1 hour.

  4. Deflate dough and turn onto a lightly floured work surface. Divide into 8 equal pieces. Form balls by folding sides into middle, pinch together, and roll with seam side down.

  5. Cover with damp towel for 10 minutes.

  6. Pinch centers of each dough ball with floured thumb and index finger and twirl around gently to make a hole, about 1 inch.

  7. Preheat oven to 425F.

  8. Bring a pot of water to a boil.

  9. Drop bagels in one at a time and boil 1 to 2 minutes per side.

  10. Place each bagel on a lightly oiled baking sheet, brush with egg wash, and add seasoning.

  11. Place in the oven and let bake for 20 minutes.
